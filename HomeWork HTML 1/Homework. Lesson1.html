Movie "The Terminator"

"The Terminator" (eng. The Terminator) - is an American cult fantasy action film by James Cameron, shot in 1984. This is the first film in the Terminator series. The film has long been on IMDb's Top 250 Movies list, as of December 12, 2021, it ranks 44th on the science fiction list.

Description

The film's success spawned a franchise consisting of several sequels, a television series, comic books, novels, and video games. In 2008, The Terminator was selected by the Library of Congress for preservation in the National Film Registry as "culturally, historically, or aesthetically significant."

The film opens with a scene of a bleak future where the war between humans and machines continues. But the main battle of this war must take place in the past, in 1984. In Los Angeles at night, a garbage truck driver witnesses strange flashes from which a naked man appears, Terminator T-800 (Arnold Schwarzenegger). This unknown finds a group of punks, whom he orders to give him his clothes. In response to the threats, the unknown person shows tremendous strength, kills the punks and takes away the clothes.
